=== FINOPS PRACTICAL: COST ANALYSIS CHALLENGE ===

You are a FinOps engineer. Your company spent $6,750 last month on Azure.
Your goal: Find waste and recommend cuts.

QUESTIONS FOR YOUR STUDENTS:

1. Which resource is the BIGGEST cost driver? Why might it be wasteful?

2. The "prod-db-main" is idle 80% of the time. What would you recommend?
   a) Delete it
   b) Right-size it (smaller instance)
   c) Use auto-scaling
   d) Move to reserved instances

3. "dev-vm-01" runs 24/7. Should it? What's a better approach?

4. If you could cut 30% of costs ($2,025), which 2-3 resources would you optimize?

5. What tags would you add to prevent this waste in the future?

BONUS: Calculate potential savings if you:
- Shut down dev-vm-01 on weekends (assume 40% savings)
- Right-size prod-db-main (assume 50% savings)
- Delete cdn-old (assume 100% savings)

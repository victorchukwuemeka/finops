Challenge questions
1. Which 3 resources are wasting money, and why?
dev-vm-01 runs 24/7 including nights and weekends — dev VMs should stop when nobody is working. prod-db-main is idle 80% of the time, meaning it is over-provisioned for its actual workload. cdn-old is deprecated and should have been deleted when its replacement went live.
2. To save $2,025 (30%), what would you cut?
Delete cdn-old ($600) + right-size prod-db-main (~$600 saved) + schedule dev-vm-01 to stop on weekends (~$320 saved) = ~$1,520. For the remaining ~$500, add auto-shutdown on weekday evenings for dev-vm-01.
